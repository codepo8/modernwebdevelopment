The Cafe

Cafe Vintage was started in late November 2010 by sisters Aysha and Nadia Allman. It was an opportunity to combine their shared love of fashion and baking and share what they had to offer with as many people as possible.

Fashion

We have a constantly changing range of clothing for kids, men and women. From hats to coats, from tiaras to sweaters, from shoes to skirts - if it is vintage, you'll find it here.

Food

All the food served in vintage cafe is freshly made and prepared on the spot. From cakes and cookies over sandwiches up to soups and pies - we have it and you are happy to come and try it out. Coffee, drinks and tea are also available. We also sell artisan bread for you to take home.

Gifts

Looking for a special gift with a vintage touch? Look no further. From postcards to crockery - we have you sorted.
